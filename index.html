Re-Style It is a pre-love fashion resale mobile app aimed at 25 to 34 years olds. Designed with style at the forefront, the app allows users to browse and purchase items as well as listing their own for sale.

Thrift is a financial responsive website and mobile app with the mission of providing a savings tool to equip everyone with the knowledge and resources to achieve their savings goals. Creating a strong brand identity, Thrift has been designed with a clean and professional interface to elicit confidence and trust from its users.

Inclusive Cooks Club

Created to address the needs of users overlooked, Inclusive Cooks Club is a recipe responsive website aimed at providing users with dietary requirements and preferences an easy way to browse and find recipes. House Mate

Created for the ever-growing shared household market, House Mate is a household organisation mobile app aimed at renters in shared households with friends or even strangers. The native app design ensures a clean interface for both Android and IOS with functionality at the heart allowing the user to complete a vast range of household organisation tasks.
