Project Overview

The objective of this project was to design a mobile app for a clothing resale brand.

The brand has a target audience of 25 to 34 year olds who want to browse and purchase preloved pieces, as well as listing and selling their own.

User Stories

I want to create a user profile so I can save my favourite styles for later and easily see previous purchases.

I want to upload my own items to sell with listings including images, descriptions and categories to help users find my items.

I want to leave reviews for sellers I have purchased from to provide helpful feedback to other users.

Images

Model images should be inspirational with a street style edge. They should feature neutral backgrounds which allow focus on the model and clothing. Images will feature a filter overlay to ensure they do not deter attention from important UI elements.

Users will have the ability to upload images when they list their products for sale. All users will be encouraged to upload imagery with a plain background to help give focus to their products and ensure the app is easy to shop.
