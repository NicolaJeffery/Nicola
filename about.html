My Story

My name is Nicola Jeffery, I graduated The Open University in 2014 with a First-Class BA(Hons) in Business Studies and Economics. For the past 8 years I have been working within the fashion retail sector as a Merchandise Planner with a focus on e-commerce. My experience within the industry has provided me with advanced knowledge of key e-commerce and online elements such as Google Analytics, conversion, demand generation and content marketing, and how to influence these to achieve success.

Whilst I have achieved great success in the industry, I found myself seeking opportunities to utilise my creative skills more and wanted to start designing the content and interfaces for the websites I was managing. I started looking into the creative field and found the UI design role which felt like the perfect fit for me. As I began studying my passion for design allowed me to create stylish and functional projects, whilst my advanced experience within e-commerce enhanced my ability to address key problems within my designs.

I take great pride in creating stylish, clean and functional interfaces and really thrive at exceeding customer expectations.
